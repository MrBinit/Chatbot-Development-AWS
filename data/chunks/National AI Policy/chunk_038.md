10.3 AI Excellence Centre
AI Excellence Centres shall be established at federal, provincial, and local levels,
as well as in universities, AI related research institutions, and educational
institutions to promote AI study, research, development, and application.
10.3.1 Functions, Duties and Powers of AI Excellence Centre
(a)  Conduct research and development in AI technology sector.
(b)  Organize training and workshops to develop AI skills for students and
researchers.
