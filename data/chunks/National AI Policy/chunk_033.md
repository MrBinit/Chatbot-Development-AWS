Chapter Three: Policy Implementation  
10. Institutional Arrangements
For the implementation of the National AI Policy, the following institutional
arrangements shall be made:
10.1 AI Regulation Council
To provide overall guidance, develop standards, and regulate the development and
use of AI, an AI Regulation Council shall be formed as follows:
(a)  Minister, Ministry of Communication and
Information Technology
– Chairperson
(b)  Secretary, Office of the Prime Mi nister and Council
of Ministers
– Member
(c)  Secretary, Ministry of Communication and
Information Technology
– Member
(d)  Secretary, Ministry of Finance  – Member
(e)  Secretary (technical), Ministry of Education, Science
and Technology
– Member
(f)  Chairperson, Nepal Telecommunications Authority  – Member
(g)  Chief Executive Officer, E-Governance Board – Member
(h)  Three subject-matter experts from the private sector
and universities (including at least one woman)
– Member
(i)  Chief, National AI Centre  –Member
